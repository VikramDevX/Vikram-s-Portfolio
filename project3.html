MakeMyTrip Website Redesign

I redesigned the ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation) website to give it a fresh, modern look while making space-related information more accessible to the public. The original website felt outdated and text-heavy, so my goal was to create a clean, visually engaging, and user-friendly design that reflects ISRO's innovation and importance in space research. I used a bold, futuristic style with improved layouts, better typography, and strong visuals to communicate ISRO's achievements clearly.


The website structure was reorganized into distinct sections such as a hero banner showcasing ISRO's missions, a highlights section for recent launches and achievements, mission details with interactive visuals, and a quick access panel for important links and resources. This restructuring ensures that users can easily find updates on missions, projects, and programs while also engaging with ISRO's vision for the future.


I designed wireframes and mockups in Figma, then developed the website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with responsiveness as a core focus. Smooth hover effects, interactive elements, and optimized images were used to improve the browsing experience across devices.
