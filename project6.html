MakeMyTrip Website Redesign

I built Vikram's Social Links as my second independent project, focusing on creating a simple yet impactful personal website. The main goal was to design a minimal, clean, and functional landing page where all my important links could be found in one place. Unlike my first independent project (GlamHub Store), this one was about combining simplicity with a personal brand identity, while still practicing my front-end development skills.


The website features a profile section at the top with my name and a short introduction, followed by neatly designed social media buttons that link to my profiles across platforms. I used a clean card-style layout with hover effects to make the design more engaging and intuitive. Responsiveness was also a key focus, ensuring the page looks and works smoothly on both desktop and mobile.


Through this project, I improved my skills in UI design and layout structuring while also learning how to align design choices with personal branding. It gave me confidence to build lightweight, effective websites that serve a clear purpose. The final outcome is a personal social link hub that works as my digital visiting card, helping me share my work and connect easily.
