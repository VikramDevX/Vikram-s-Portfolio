MakeMyTrip Website Redesign

I redesigned the Cafe New York (Mumbai) website with the goal of giving the café a modern and welcoming online identity. Many café websites I researched felt either outdated or cluttered, so my focus was on building a design that was minimal yet engaging, where the café's unique vibe could stand out. To achieve this, I crafted a clean layout with warm colors, elegant typography, and high-quality visuals that capture the cozy atmosphere of the café.


The website is structured into clear, user-friendly sections including a hero banner, an About section that introduces the café, a menu highlights area showcasing popular food and beverages, a gallery that reflects the ambience, and a contact/location section for easy customer navigation. This structured flow ensures visitors can quickly learn about the café, explore offerings, and connect without any confusion.


I started with wireframes and a style guide in Figma, then developed the site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with a strong focus on responsiveness. The design includes smooth hover effects, clean layouts, and optimized visuals to ensure performance across devices. The end result is a professional and user-friendly café website that reflects the brand's warmth, enhances customer experience, and strengthens the café's digital presence.
