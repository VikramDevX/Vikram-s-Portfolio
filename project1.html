MakeMyTrip Website Redesign

I redesigned the MakeMyTrip website with the goal of making the travel booking journey cleaner, faster, and more engaging. I then developed the site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, ensuring it was fully responsive with smooth interactions like hover effects and visual overlays. The design emphasizes usability and performance, with optimized images and a mobile-first approach.
